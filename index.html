Projects by Alan Zatarain

MACOP-MX

Modification of a base system to display a map and statistics of obesity indices in Mexico imported with an Excel format. Georg-August-Universität Göttingen, Universidad Autónoma “Benito Juárez” de Oaxaca and Universidad de Guadalajara received a fast and reliable system able to export the map and data tables as PDF or Excel, filtering records by date, gender, state and kind of indicator.

Languages: Python (back-end) and JavaScript (front-end). Frameworks: Django (back-end) and Angular.js (front-end). Database: PostgreSQL. Deploy: Digital Ocean
